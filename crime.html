iSafe

About the Project

It Contain Two mobile app - one for general public and the other for police. People can check unsafe area on different routes from source to destination,if any and can choose safer path. If the person is in unsafe area,S/he can share live location,real time mic and camera with the prime contact. In case of any accident scan send an SOS message using always on gesture. In police side app - Police will go to the crime spot and update the location with crime into the database.
